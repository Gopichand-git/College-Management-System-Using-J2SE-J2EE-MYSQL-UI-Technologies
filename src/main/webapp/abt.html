About Gopichand Engineering College

GEC is an autonomous institute permanently affiliated to JNTU Kakinada, accredited with NAAC ‘A’ grade. The college is known for academic excellence, state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and an industry-oriented curriculum.

It has consistently produced top performers and nurtured thousands of students who are now thriving in global careers.
